## Sizing Guidelines

| Context | Recommended Size | Variant |
|---------|-----------------|---------|
| Website hero/banner | 400-600px wide | Full Seal or Lockup |
| Website footer | 150-200px wide | Lockup |
| Email signature | 200-300px wide | Lockup |
| Social media profile | 200-400px | Full Seal |
| Favicon | 32x32 or 180x180 | Minimal Flag |
| Print (business card) | 1 inch minimum | Full Seal |
| Presentation slide | 300-500px | Full Seal or Dark |

---

## Do's

- Display the badge at a readable size (minimum 80px wide for seal, 150px for lockup)
- Use the provided variants -- do not recreate the badge
- Include attribution when using the open badge: "Created by Just In Time AI"
- Link back to the GitHub repo or jitai.co when possible
- Use the appropriate variant for your background (light bg = standard, dark bg = dark variant)
- Use the #FirstMover hashtag on social media

## Don'ts

- Do not modify the badge colors, proportions, or text
- Do not stretch, skew, or distort the badge
- Do not place the badge on busy backgrounds that reduce readability
- Do not use the certified badge without completing certification
- Do not remove the attribution requirement (open badge)
- Do not use the badge to imply endorsement by Just In Time AI for products not covered by your directory entry
- Do not add effects (drop shadows, glows, 3D transforms) to the badge

## Attribution

### Open Badge (Required)

When displaying the open badge, include one of these attribution formats:

**Text:**
```
#FirstMover badge by Just In Time AI (jitai.co)
```

**HTML:**
```html
<a href="https://github.com/dstolts/FirstMover">#FirstMover</a> badge by <a href="https://jitai.co">Just In Time AI</a>
```

**Markdown:**
```markdown
[#FirstMover](https://github.com/dstolts/FirstMover) badge by [Just In Time AI](https://jitai.co)
```

### Certified Badge

No public attribution required beyond displaying the badge itself. The certification speaks for itself.
