# #FirstMover Certified

The #FirstMover Certified badge is a validated credential that confirms your first-mover claim has been independently reviewed by Just In Time AI.

---

## Why Get Certified?

- **Credibility** -- an independent review of your first-mover claim
- **Visibility** -- listed in the Certified section of the #FirstMover directory
- **Assets** -- receive the certified badge (gold ring design) for your website, marketing, and social media
- **Recognition** -- stand out from open badge holders with a verified credential

---

## Certification Process

1. **Apply** -- Complete the application form at [jitai.co](https://jitai.co) with:
   - Company name and URL
   - Industry vertical
   - Description of the gap you filled
   - Evidence of first-mover timing (launch date, market analysis, competitor landscape)
   - Links to the live product or service

2. **Pay** -- One-time certification fee of **$1,000** via Stripe

3. **Review** -- 20-minute review call with Just In Time AI ([Schedule via Calendly](https://calendly.com/danstolts/20min))

4. **Approval** -- Upon approval:
   - Your entry is added to the Certified directory
   - You receive the #FirstMover Certified badge assets (seal, minimal, dark, lockup)
   - You sign the [Certified License Agreement](CERTIFIED-LICENSE.md)

5. **Rejection** -- If your application does not meet the criteria, you may submit a rebuttal within 60 days with new or additional evidence. No additional fee required. The certification fee is non-refundable

---

## Dispute and Revocation

Certification can be disputed by any third party. If a dispute is filed:

1. The dispute is submitted to Just In Time AI with supporting evidence
2. The certifiee is notified and given **30 days** to provide a rebuttal with counter-evidence
3. Just In Time AI reviews both sides and makes a final determination
4. If the claim is upheld, certification remains active
5. If the claim is found to be false, **certification is revoked** -- the certifiee is removed from the directory and must immediately stop using the certified badge

**There are no refunds upon revocation or rejection.** The $1,000 fee covers the cost of the review process regardless of outcome.

Full legal terms: [CERTIFIED-LICENSE.md](CERTIFIED-LICENSE.md)

## Certification Criteria

- **Real product** -- You have a live, functioning AI product or service (not vaporware, not a prototype)
- **Real gap** -- You solved an identifiable problem in your industry
- **First-mover evidence** -- You were first or among the first in your specific market. If competitors exist, you can demonstrate you were there first
- **AI-powered** -- AI is a core component of your solution, not a marketing label

## Governance

**Current model (launch):** Just In Time AI reviews and approves all certification applications. As the creator of the #FirstMover standard, we bring cross-industry pattern recognition and the judgment to evaluate first-mover claims fairly.

**Future model (at 10-20 certified members):** Transition to a peer review process where existing certified #FirstMovers participate in reviewing new applications. This scales credibility without creating a bottleneck.

We are transparent about this evolution. The community will shape how certification governance matures.
